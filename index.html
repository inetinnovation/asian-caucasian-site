// About

The Band
With That Name

Yes, we know what you're thinking. We thought it too. Then someone said "what if that was actually a band?" and here we are — a legitimate pop project out of Indianapolis, Indiana, with a name that makes every first interaction interesting.

Asian Caucasian makes pop music — the kind that sneaks into your head during a commute and refuses to leave. We're self-aware enough to find the whole thing funny and serious enough to make songs you'll actually want to play again.

Currently ranked #7 on the local Indianapolis pop charts on ReverbNation. Gunning for #1. Probably.
